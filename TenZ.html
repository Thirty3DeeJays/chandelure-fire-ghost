TenZ

Valorant's Golden Child

Every league has a "most popular player" and for VCT, that player is TenZ. Tyson Van Ngo, better known as TenZ, is a Canadian professional Valorant player for Sentinels. He is arguably one of the Valorant greats, amassing a kill count of over 4500 plus more since this data set is not updated to the latest event.

The Duelist Role

The most popular players on most rosters is the player who is on duelist, as they are the ones who get into the bomb site and have the tools and abilities necessary to take out the opponent. Some of the most popular characters, like Jett, Raze and Reyna, are duelists and therefore, when people watch the players play those roles, they tend to gravitate more towards those players.

TenZ on Duelist

Tenz plays for Sentinels, the most popular team in VCT Americas and one of the most popular teams in the world. In 2021, it seemed like Sentinels couldn't be stopped. They won Masters Reykjav√≠k, which is sort of like an in-season tournament that only the best teams from each region qualify for. Sentinels won this event in convincing fashion with, who else, but TenZ at the forefront of it all. However, after this, Sentinels never really got back up to the hype. Reflected in his kills and rounds played, TenZ was playing less Valorant due to a lack of qualifying for events as well as doing worse on Jett, his main character for most of his career up to that point. In 2023, Sentinels signed zekken, another duelist player, and tried to run the double duelist composition to little success. They even benched TenZ in 2023 due to worse and worse performances. It seemed like this era was over for TenZ

TenZ on Flex

In 2024, Sentinels signed johnqt to IGL (in-game leader/captain) for the team, as well as picked sentinel player Zellsis. (Quick aside, there is a role in the game known as Sentinel, which has no affiliation with the team.) TenZ was moved over to a flex role, but where he really thrived was with one character, Omen. Omen is a controller, meaning he is able to smoke off areas of the map to hide player and/or information from the enemy team. And finally, it seemed like TenZ had found his mojo back. TenZ also plays characters Kayo and Gekko, two initiators, who's job it is to set up the team with flash grenades and information gathering tools to see where the enemy could be. Sentinels went on to defeat Gen.G in Masters Madrid, where TenZ sits atop of the world as Valorant's Golden Child.
