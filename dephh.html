dephh

Professional Valorant's Dinosaur

Dephh is the last player I am doing for this project. I wanted to look at someone who was 1) much older and 2) not in the game anymore. Rory Jackson is a retired British/American player.

The Early Days (65 million BC)

Dephh as a player is very intresting. Dephh was 28 when Valorant came out. He was a Counter Strike professional when he decided to join Valorant. He joined with Dignitas in 2020, and was the IGL for Dignitas until they released their roster in 2021. There dephh was then picked up by XSET. There he played well, also being the IGL and helping out a young zekken with his game.

Franchising

XSET did not recieve a spot in franchising, meaning the players were released and could sign where ever they wanted. A lot of XSET chose to sign with Sentinels. This was a big move for Sentinels. They were already one of the most popular teams, and to get some of the best players from one of the best Americas teams? This would be Sentinels year to win it all.

Narrator: They did not win it all

Sentinels severely underperformed in 2023, and most of the blame was put on to dephh. He played poorly, and IGL'ed even worse. He was eventually benched during the 2023 season. Dephh ended his Valorant career with a whimper, as he retired from the game of Valorant in July of 2023. He returned to Counter Strike, where he is currently playing.

Why a dinosaur?

Dephh just couldn't keep up with the game of Valorant. It's a tactical shooter, but it's also a faster paced game with more colors. Dephh and his old man eyes couldn't keep up with it. And that's okay! Valorant isn't for everyone! He was Professional Valorant's Dinosaur.
