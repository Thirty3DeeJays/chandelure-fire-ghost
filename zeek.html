zeek

Professional Valorant's Disappearing Act

When first coming up with the idea for this project, another idea I wanted to discuss was looking at a player who really fell off. Like someone who was on top of the world and just never made it back. I went to the first ever Champs in 2021 and looked at the roster that won. From there, I got to zeek. Aleksander "zeek" Zygmunt is a Polish player who is currently playing for GoNext Esports.

Acend ESports

Acend is a EMEA team that was just kinda decent. They weren't the best in EMEA, but they could hold their own. However, during the season, they picked up a couple of key players. One of those players being zeek. Zeek was their duelist, aka, they guy you're relying on to get the most kills. And zeek was that guy, having a 1.09 KDR and a .76 KPR while on Acend.

The boys win champs!

Acend made it to Champs in Berlin. The road was long and tough for Acend. But they made it out of their group and was able to take home the gold. Zeek played exceptionally well here. Being the duelist and the game being in its infancy, the squad had some tactics that may not fly in today's game.

2022, the boys do worse

2022 was not good for Acend. They didn't qualify for any international events as well as placing incredibly poorly at both stages of Challengers. Zeek left Acend and was picked up by Team Heretics.

Team Heretics/The boys are gone

Zeek's stint with Team Heretics lasted for about 5 months, which is a long time. However, his performance felt incredibly lackluster. He was a shell of his former self. His kills were down, and his KDR took a big hit going to a .98. Having a negative KDR as a pro player is a death sentence, and zeek was moved to an inactive position. Currently zeek plays for a tier 2 team. His days of being the best in the world is over. He is like a magician, making people believe that what they are seeing is true when in reality, its all an illusion. Zeek truly is Valorant's Disappeating Act.
